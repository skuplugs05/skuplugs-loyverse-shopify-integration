# skuplugs-loyverse-shopify-integration
[**Loyverse Shopify integration by SKUPlugs**](https://skuplugs.com/loyverse-shopify-integration/) connects your in-store POS and online store to work as one unified system. It automatically syncs products, inventory levels, pricing, and orders between Loyverse and Shopify in real time. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces errors, and ensures accurate stock management across all sales channels. Retailers can manage operations more efficiently, improve customer experience, and scale their business with confidence. With secure cloud-based technology and easy setup, SKUPlugs provides a reliable solution for businesses looking to streamline retail operations and maintain consistency between offline and online sales.
